# Onboarding interviews — synthesis

Source: 14 interviews, Mar–May 2026. Raw transcripts in `sources/` (not committed).

## Recurring friction
- **Day one is spent waiting.** Eleven of fourteen mentioned credential provisioning
  as the first blocker. Median time to first commit: 3.5 days.
- **The runbook is trusted more than the docs**, because it is dated and the docs
  are not. Two interviewees said they never opened the docs site at all.
- **Nobody could name the on-call escalation path** without looking it up.

## Contradiction to resolve
Interview 6 claims the staging reset runs nightly; interview 11 says weekly.
Neither was checked against the schedule itself — flagged, not resolved.
