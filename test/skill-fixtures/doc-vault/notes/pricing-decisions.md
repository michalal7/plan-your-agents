## 2026-04-02 — Seat minimum raised to 5
Evidence: churn analysis, deck p.12. Accounts below 5 seats churned at 3.1× the rate
and generated 61% of support load.
Dissent recorded: sales argued this closes the door on solo consultants. Unresolved.

## 2026-02-18 — Annual discount capped at 15%
Evidence: margin model v4. Anything above 15% put renewals below target margin.
Supersedes the 2025-11 decision that allowed 20% case by case.
